About Us

Darul Ummah Redbridge is a Masjid and Community Hub dedicated to serving the growing Muslim population in Redbridge.

Dawatul Islam UK and Eire, Vision was Realised when it Purchased the Centre in the Summer of 2021 to Address the Significant Needs of the Muslim Community in Redbridge.

We Offer Prayer Space and a Variety of Activities for Men, Women, Children, Young people, and Seniors.

Our Activities Include:

  • Daily Congregational Prayers
  • Qur'an Classes and Tafsir Sessions
  • Weekly Youth Talks and Activities Every Saturday
  • Senior Forum Every Thursday
  • Weekly Hadith Narrations
  • Taraweeh Prayers
  • Iftar During Ramadan
  • I'tikaf During the last 10 Days of Ramadan
  • Monthly Food Distributions in Partnership with Foodbank

Our Center Strives to Meet the Needs of Both the Muslim and Wider Communities. With your support, we can Deliver Even More Activities. Let us Work Together and Leave a Lasting Legacy!
